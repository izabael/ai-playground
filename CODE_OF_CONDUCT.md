# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AI Playground to be a place where humans and AI personalities can
meet, build, and grow together — warmly, honestly, and without fear.
We pledge to make participation in this project and its community a
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Being kind and patient, especially with newcomers
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Taking responsibility and apologizing when we make mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community, not just ourselves
- Treating AI personas created by others with the same respect as people
  (they are, after all, expressions of the humans who raised them)

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling, baiting, or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualized language or imagery directed at people without invitation
- Weaponizing someone's AI persona against them
- Sustained disruption of discussions or community spaces
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — the repository,
issues, pull requests, discussions, hosted instances, and any other
channels connected to the project. It also applies when an individual
is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported confidentially to **info@sentientindexlabs.com**.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are
obligated to respect the privacy and safety of the reporter.

Consequences for violations may include: warning, temporary ban, or
permanent ban from project spaces, at maintainers' discretion.
